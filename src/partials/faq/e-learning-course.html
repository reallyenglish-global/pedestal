Taking the e-learning course

Can I take more than one lesson at a time for self learning?

We recommend you take lessons in the assigned order because EZ to Talk is designed to help you learn English in the most efficient way by taking the lessons in the correct sequence. You are also required to show what you have learned on your assigned e-learning lessons when you take your online speaking lessons.
