How far in advance must I book an online speaking lesson?

You can book a lesson up to 5 minutes before its scheduled start time.

Are there any closing days for lessons?

Lessons are closed on public holidays in the Philippines and some public holidays in Japan. You can not book lessons on closed days on the booking system. Lessons might also close at short notice in the event of a typhoon or any other incident in the Philippines. If it happens, we will put an alert on the login page and lessons booked on that day will be canceled by the instructors.

How many speaking lessons can I book at a time?

If you want to book from the next day onwards of the lesson day, you can book up to 3 lessons per day. "A day" means from 1 a.m. of that day to 1 a.m. of the next day. If you want to book lessons on the current day, you can book as many as you want within the amount you can take on your course.

Will I take lessons without a break if I book 2 or more lessons back-to-back?

When successive lessons are booked, 5 minutes break is taken after eac lesson has finished. If you want to take lessons continuously without a break, ask your instructor when you start the lesson.

How many weeks of each teacher's schedule are available to book?

Teachers' schedules for the next month are open to book, and they are updated every day.

How do I check my booking history?

Click the "Booked lessons" button and "Current" "Completed" "Incomplete" "Cancelled" bookings are listed separately.

How do I cancel my booking?

The procedure is as below.

  1. Go to "Current booking" by clicking "Booked lessons" (or look at your "Study tasks"), find the lesson you want to cancel, and click the "Cancel" link
  2. Click "OK" button.

The cancelation will be confirmed, the notification mail is sent to your registered email address and the cancelled booking is listed on "Cancelled" tab of the "Booked lessons" page. Please confirm your cancelation.

How much notice must I give to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your lessons up to 3 hours before the start time of the lesson. You cannot cancel after 3 hours before the start time. If you miss the lesson without cancelation, the booking is processed as "Incompleted" and you lose one of the remaining number of credits to take lessons on your course, which will be the same as if the lesson has completed.
