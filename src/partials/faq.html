Preparation

How should I take lessons?

<<If your e-learning course name does NOT include the word "FULL">>

Refer to Get started (link to guidance page).

<<If your e-learning course name includes the word "FULL">>

You can take all the e-learning course lessons besides the EZ to Talk lessons. Please check the following for the effective learning of both courses.

  • Preparation
    1. Go back to MyPage and click the "start lesson" button of the e-learning course.
    2. If you are required to create a Learning Path, take the Diagnostic Test and create your Learning Path by following the instructions on the screen.

  • How to take the EZ to Talk lessons

    • Get started shows the basic process to take the lessons. However, please follow the process shown ina Step XX (how to proceed with your self-learning) of Get started.

    On Study tasks, find the lesson you need and download the PDF teaching materials. Mmake a note of the lesson title and whether it is Grammar, Reading or Listening.

    Go back to MyPage and click the "start lesson" button on your e-learning course.

    Click "All activities" on the top of the page.

    Click the correct tab for Grammar, Reading or Listening on the top right of the page.

    Search for the same lesson by entering the lesson title, (which you made a note of beforehand) into the search bar.

    The lesson you need will be listed, so start the lesson. If you have already taken that lesson on the following e-learning course, we recommend taking the same lesson again as preparation or follow-up to your online speaking lessons.

  • How to take the lessons of the e-learning course

    • You can take a lesson from either your Learning Path or from All Lessons. The Learning Path is your own personalized program of recommended lessons. All Lessons is where you can choose a lesson on a topic that interests you.

    Go back to MyPage and click the "start lesson" button on your e-learning course.

    Take lessons in the order designed by your Learning Path
